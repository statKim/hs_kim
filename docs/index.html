About

I am a Ph.D. student from Department of Statistics at Chung-Ang University, under the supervision of Prof. Yaeji Lim. I am also a member of the Multivariate Statistics Lab. Prior to that, I received my M.A. in Statistics and B.A. in Applied Statistics at Chung-Ang University.

My research interests include dimension reduction, functional data analysis and high-dimensional data analysis. Currently, my main research work includes robust principal component analysis for partially observed functional data.

I love R coding and making documents using Rmarkdown. I sometimes write on my personal blog about Statistics, R, etc.
